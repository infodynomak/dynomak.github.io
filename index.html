About Dynomak

Dynomak provides professional, independent third-party inspection and quality assurance services to ensure that your products, materials, and processes meet international standards. Our experienced engineers and inspectors help clients reduce risk, maintain compliance, and verify quality throughout manufacturing and delivery stages.

Our Services

Pre-Production Inspection

Evaluate raw materials, components, and supplier readiness before production starts — minimizing costly issues later in the process.

During Production Inspection

Monitor manufacturing in progress to confirm compliance with design and specifications, helping to prevent defects early.

Pre-Shipment Inspection

Verify finished goods before dispatch to ensure they meet quality, quantity, packaging, and labeling requirements.

Loading Supervision

Ensure that products are properly loaded and secured before shipment to reduce damage and mix-ups.

Factory Audits

Assess manufacturing capabilities, quality systems, and working conditions to qualify and monitor suppliers.
